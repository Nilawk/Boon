Boons of the Unknown Roadmap Team Tokenomics

Welcome to Boons of the Unknown!

Embark on an epic adventure in our 2D roguelike dungeon crawler. Explore the depths of mysterious dungeons, face fearsome enemies, and uncover hidden treasures. Every dungeon you delve into is unique and procedurally generated, ensuring a different experience every time you play.

Boons of the Unknown is built on the Polygon blockchain, allowing for seamless and secure transactions. Earn unique NFTs and trade them with other players, or use them to gain an edge in your dungeon crawling exploits.

Are you ready to brave the unknown and uncover the boons that await? Start your adventure today!

Roadmap

  • Q2 2024: Conceptualization and initial game design
  • Q3 2024: Development of basic game mechanics and first level design
  • Q3 2024: Integration with Polygon blockchain and development of NFT assets
  • Q4 2024: Testing phase and refinement of game mechanics
  • Q4 2024: Launch of beta version for select users
  • Q4 2024: Incorporation of user feedback and final adjustments
  • Q1 2025: Official game launch
  • Q2 2025: Continuous updates and introduction of new NFT assets

Team

  • Project Lead (WaKe): Responsible for overall project management and coordination.
  • Game Designer (Kronkite): In charge of designing the game mechanics and user experience.
  • Blockchain Developer (Venic): Integrates the game with the Polygon blockchain and develops the NFT assets.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 BOON Tokens
  • Game Rewards: 40% of total supply. These tokens will be earned by players as they progress through the game.
  • Development Fund: 20% of total supply. These tokens will be used to fund ongoing development and improvements to the game.
  • Marketing & Partnerships: 15% of total supply. These tokens will be used for marketing campaigns and strategic partnerships.
  • Team Allocation: 15% of total supply. These tokens will be distributed among the team members as compensation for their work.
  • Community Airdrops: 10% of total supply. These tokens will be distributed to the community through airdrops and other promotional events.
